Unable to log in to WeChat

1. Forgot WeChat password
1.1 Use your linked information to log in to WeChat (i.e. linked mobile number, email, QQ, Facebook)

     ② Unable to log in to WeChat via linked information? Click here.

1.2 You haven't linked your mobile/email/QQ/Facebook to your account? Click here.

1.3 If password retrieval was not successful, click here.

2. Unable to receive SMS verification code or password reset email
2.1 After entering your linked mobile number, if you're still unable to receive an SMS with the verification code, click here.
2.2 After entering your linked email address, if you're still unable to receive an email with the verification code, click here.

3. Using a new device to log in for the first time

3.2 Unable to verify your identity to log in? Click here.
